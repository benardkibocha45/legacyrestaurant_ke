About Us

Welcome to Legacy Restaurant

Founded in 2013, Legacy Restaurant KE is a family-owned gem in Karuri, Kenya, celebrating the richness of Kenyan cuisine with a modern twist. Our name reflects our commitment to preserving culinary traditions while innovating for today’s palate.

Every dish is crafted with Love at First Bite in mind—using locally sourced ingredients and recipes passed down through generations. From our signature nyama choma to hearty mukimo, we invite you to taste the legacy.
