About Us

Welcome to Legacy Restaurant

Nestled in Karuri, Legacy Restaurant is a tribute to Kenya’s rich culinary heritage. Our dishes tell stories—of family, tradition, and the joy of sharing a meal.

We believe in Love at First Bite. Whether it’s our tender mbuzi choma, creamy mukimo, or decadent desserts, every dish is crafted to delight.
